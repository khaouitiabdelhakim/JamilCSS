JamilCSS includes 22 color families, each with shades from 50 (lightest) to 950 (darkest). Shade numbers map to Tailwind-like hex values.

## Families

gray, blue, red, green, yellow, orange, pink, purple, indigo, teal, cyan, slate, rose, sky, lime, emerald, violet, fuchsia, amber, zinc, neutral, stone
